ABOUT GEENIUS SPORTS FEDERATION, INDIA

GEENIUS SPORTS FEDERATION, INDIA (GSF INDIA)

  • Genius Sports Federation (GSF) is an authorized and recognized sports organization.
  • We are committed to discovering and nurturing sports talent across India.
  • Our goal is to awaken the sports spirit in every child and guide them toward athletic excellence.
  • GSF is a non-profit and non-private initiative focused entirely on athlete development.
  • Through its website, GSF aims to digitally connect with coaches, players, and academies across India. By registering online, you can stay updated on matches, events, and opportunities offered by GSF
