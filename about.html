ABOUT GEENIUS SPORTS FEDERATION, INDIA

GEENIUS SPORTS FEDERATION, INDIA (GSF INDIA)

  • Genius Sports Federation (GSF) is an authorized and recognized sports organization.
  • We are committed to discovering and nurturing sports talent across India.
  • Our goal is to awaken the sports spirit in every child and guide them toward athletic excellence.
  • GSF is a non-profit and non-private initiative focused entirely on athlete development.
  • Through its website, GSF aims to digitally connect with coaches, players, and academies across India. By registering online, you can stay updated on matches, events, and opportunities offered by GSF

GEENIUS SPORTS FEDERATION, INDIA (GSF INDIA)

Our Foundation

Mission • Vision • Objectives • Aims

1

Mission

  • To inspire every child to realize their athletic potential
  • To develop discipline, dedication, and character through sports
  • To provide equal opportunities and professional coaching to all young athletes
2

Vision

  • To help Indian athletes reach the international stage, including the Olympics
  • To build a sports culture in India where excellence, ethics, and energy thrive
  • To act as a stepping stone from grassroots to greatness for every athlete
3

Objectives

  • Identify young talent in both urban and rural areas
  • Provide professional coaching, mentorship, and support
  • Organize tournaments, training camps, and talent hunts across India
  • Collaborate with schools, academies, and government bodies
  • Promote physical education and fitness awareness at every level
4

Aims

  • To build a generation of skilled, confident, and proud Indian athletes
  • To create a transparent and accessible sports ecosystem
  • To be a trusted guide and partner in an athlete's journey from beginner to champion

SPORTS AUTHORITY OF INDIA

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

  • Talent Scouting at micro level & Nurturing talent towards excellence.
  • Training & International Exposure.
  • Support Training with Scientific & Sports Equipment and scientific personnel.
  • Monitor and enhance Performance with a scientific evaluation system.
  • Training and preparation of National teams.
  • Sports Infrastructure Development & Maintenance.
  • Maintenance and up gradation of 4 stadia complexes and a shooting range in Delhi.
  • To produce coaches and physical educationists of high caliber in different disciplines of sports to broad base sports.
  • To implement various schemes of the MYAS e.g. Khelo India, Assistance to NSF, TOPS, FIT India.
