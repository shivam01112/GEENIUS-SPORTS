Selection Process

Online Registration

Complete your registration through the GSF Portal with all required details and documents.

Document Verification

Submit and verify your ID, age proof, and sports certificates for eligibility confirmation.

Physical Fitness Test

Demonstrate your physical capabilities through speed, agility, and endurance assessments.

Skills Assessment

Showcase your sport-specific skills and techniques relevant to your chosen discipline.

Final Interview

Interact with coaches and selectors to discuss your goals and commitment to the sport.

Results & Selection

Receive your results and get shortlisted for advanced training camps and programs.
